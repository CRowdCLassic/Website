Crowd Coin ( CRC )

CrowdCoin is a proof of work based crypto currency that has been built from the ground up on our own blockchain technology, allowing us to adapt and make changes for the greater good of both the platform and its underlying currency, using masternodes to provide added stability to both the network and coin value. The purpose of CrowdCoin is to serve as a currency to be used in transactions between users on the CrowdCoin platform which is now under development.

We are are developing a platform accessible to everyone, on which we offer information and training courses giving insight into crypto currencies from an economic, tech and legislative point of view as well as offering advisory content and real-time statistics and data related to investments within the crypto and blockchain universe. All of this with a built in fully AML/KYC compliant crypto currency exchange platform.

The CrowdCoin team is not fixed and is continuously expanding to meet the demand of our users, always keeping our eyes and ears open towards the community and their input, suggestions, opinions. We make use of a bounty/reward system to create financial room for outsourcing tasks that could improve our platform.
